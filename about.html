Health-Assist

Empowering rural healthcare with technology.

Access to quality healthcare remains a significant challenge in rural areas due to inadequate infrastructure, a shortage of medical professionals, and limited financial resources. The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that nearly half of the global population lacks essential health services.

Our project, "Health Assist Camp for Rural Areas Using Machine Learning (ML)," seeks to address these barriers by developing an innovative platform powered by ML technology. This platform will assist healthcare workers in rural health camps by diagnosing common health conditions, predicting risks, and providing personalized treatment recommendations. The system utilizes a chatbot to interact with users, enabling quick symptom input, while an advanced machine learning model analyzes the data to predict diseases.

Based on the diagnosis, the platform also offers recommendations on diet and medicine tailored to the disease, ensuring that patients receive timely and accurate care. By empowering healthcare professionals with an intelligent, easy-to-use tool, we aim to improve healthcare delivery in rural areas, ultimately bridging the gap in access to essential medical services.
